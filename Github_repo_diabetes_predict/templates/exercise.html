Why You Should Do Exercise

Exercise helps people lose weight and lower the risk of some diseases. Yoga For Diabetes

Choose Your Yoga & Focus

Surya Namaskar

While talking about yoga for diabetes, the first pose that is extremely beneficial for diabetic patients is sun salutations. It is a perfect way to get your heart rate up and stretch your entire body..

Dhanurasana

Bow pose is a cure to fatigue. It strengthens your abdominal muscles, reduces constipation, and helps in regulating the pancreas. It is high in recommendations to balance the blood sugar level.

Paschimottanasana

Seated forward bend is an excellent option for people with diabetes. It controls diabetes, reduces blood pressure, and balances insulin levels in your blood.

sarvangasana

Supported shoulderstand, this inversion may help improve circulation and stimulate the thyroid gland. It can also help calm the mind and relieve stress.

Bhujangasana

When your triceps brachii, spinal extensors, and quadriceps muscles work together, it enhances muscle strength. Then, it ultimately lowers blood pressure and blood sugar.

Shavasana

Corpse pose is the final step in yoga for diabetes. You may start with any yoga asana, but you must finish with a corpse pose. It has definite impacts on lowering blood sugar, balancing blood pressure, calming your body and mind
